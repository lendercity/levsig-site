High-Risk Nature of Leveraged ETFs

Leveraged ETFs are highly volatile, complex financial instruments. You can lose your entire investment rapidly. Never trade leveraged ETFs with capital you cannot afford to lose entirely.

LevSig LLC publishes signals that frequently involve leveraged Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs). Before trading these instruments you should understand:

  • Daily rebalancing causes compounding decay that erodes value in sideways or volatile markets, even when the underlying index is flat or rising.
  • Leveraged ETFs are designed for short-term swing trading, not long-term holding. Holding them beyond the intended timeframe significantly increases risk of loss.
  • A 2× or 3× leveraged ETF does not deliver exactly 2× or 3× the return of its underlying index over any period longer than a single trading day.
